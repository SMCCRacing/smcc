About Us

Established in 1990, SMCC has been a hub for model car racing enthusiasts for over three decades. We’re proud to be a welcoming, community-driven club that brings together both newcomers and experienced racers. At SMCC, our focus is on fun, friendship, and fostering a love for the hobby. We offer a relaxed and inclusive atmosphere where children and adults can enjoy racing together, learn new skills, and make lasting memories. While we do race competitively, we group racers by ability—so whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned pro, you’ll always have a fair and exciting race. Come along and see why so many people keep coming back to SMCC!
