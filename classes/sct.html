Short Course Trucks

About This Class

Realistic-looking 2WD short course trucks on off-road tracks. Great fun and a fan favorite thanks to their large size and dramatic race action.

Rules

Any 1/10 chassis is allowed with any hop ups.

No restrictions.

No restrictions.

13.5T brushless max. Non-regulars with lower turn motors must notify race control and reduce speed. No points will be awarded.

Schumacher narrow mini pin in yellow compound recommended:
Front: U6793
Rear: U6794
No tyre additive permitted.

2S 7.4v Li-Po or 6 cell 7.2v NiMH only. No Li-HV charging beyond 7.4v. Random checks will occur.

Setup Tips

  • Motor: 10.5T brushless motors recommended for controlled power.
  • Ride Height: Set around 25–30mm. Trucks should sit high but not roll excessively.
  • Tyres: Mini pins work well on carpet; trim side pins to reduce traction roll.
  • Shocks: 40wt front / 35wt rear with stiff springs to support weight and body roll.
  • Chassis: 2WD handles more predictably, 4WD provides more grip and steering.
  • Body Shell: Use lightweight SCT bodies — trim rear fenders if body rub occurs.
