1/10 Off-Road Buggies

About This Class

This class includes 1/10th scale 2WD and 4WD electric buggies. Racing is off-road on grass and dirt tracks with jumps, bumps, and challenging terrain.

Rules

Any 1/10 chassis is allowed with any hop ups.

No restrictions.

No restrictions.

13.5T brushless max. Non-regulars with lower turn motors must notify race control and reduce speed. No points will be awarded.

Schumacher narrow mini pin in yellow compound recommended:
Front: U6793
Rear: U6794
No tyre additive permitted.

2S 7.4v Li-Po or 6 cell 7.2v NiMH only. No Li-HV charging beyond 7.4v. Random checks will occur.

Setup Tips

  • Motor: 13.5T brushless motors give a good balance of power and control.
  • Tyres: Schumacher yellow compound mini pins are recommended for carpet grip.
  • Ride Height: Aim for around 18–20mm; adjust to control traction and roll.
  • Shocks: Use 30–40wt oil depending on front/rear balance and jumps.
  • Camber & Toe: Front toe-out and 1–2° rear camber improve handling on tight tracks.
  • Differentials: Greased ball diff or gear diff with light oil help maintain corner speed.
