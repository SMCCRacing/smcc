Tamiya Tourers

About This Class

A fun, entry-level class featuring Tamiya TT-01, TT-02, and similar chassis. This class focuses on close, affordable racing for all levels of experience.

Rules

Any 1/10 chassis is allowed with any hop ups.

No restrictions.

No restrictions.

13.5T brushless max. Non-regulars with lower turn motors must notify race control and reduce speed. No points will be awarded.

Schumacher narrow mini pin in yellow compound recommended:
Front: U6793
Rear: U6794
No tyre additive permitted.

2S 7.4v Li-Po or 6 cell 7.2v NiMH only. No Li-HV charging beyond 7.4v. Random checks will occur.

Setup Tips

  • Chassis: Use TT-01 or TT-02 with standard gearing and bearings.
  • Motor: Only Tamiya Torque Tuned motors allowed — keep clean and properly soldered.
  • Tyres: Any rubber tyre permitted. Mini pins or carpet-specific tyres recommended.
  • Battery: 2S Li-Po with hard case or 6-cell NiMH. Li-Fe batteries are also allowed.
  • Shocks: Oil-filled CVA shocks help improve stability. Use medium oil and basic spring tuning.
  • Weight: Aim for around 1500g. Add weights low in chassis if too light.
