How Race Days Work

At Sevenoaks Model Car Club, race days are designed to be friendly, competitive, and enjoyable for all drivers.

Typical Schedule

  • 08:00 - Venue opens, setup begins
  • 08:45 - Track open for practice
  • 09:30 - Driver briefing
  • 10:00 - Qualifying rounds begin
  • 13:00 - Finals start
  • 15:30 - Pack down and close

General Rules

  • All drivers must marshal the race after their own
  • Only 1/10th scale electric vehicles are allowed
  • Lipo batteries must be charged in lipo-safe bags
  • No shouting or aggressive behavior will be tolerated
  • Have fun and help others!
