1/10 Buggy Class

Buggy racing is one of the most exciting and dynamic RC classes at SMCC. This class is open to both 2WD and 4WD 1/10 buggies, making it a popular choice for drivers who enjoy jumping, sliding, and wheel-to-wheel racing action.

Car Example

Here’s a look at a typical 1/10 buggy setup used at SMCC — lightweight, powerful, and race-ready.

Rules

ChassisAny 2WD or 4WD 1/10 chassis is allowed with any hop-ups.
ESCNo restrictions.
ServoNo restrictions.
Motor13.5T brushless max. Non-regulars using lower turn motors must inform race control and limit speed to 13.5T equivalent. No championship points if not compliant.
GearingNo restrictions.
TyresControl tyre: Schumacher Mini Spike Yellow Compound
Front: U6793
Rear: U6794

No tyre additive allowed under any circumstances.
BodyshellAny 1/10 buggy shell is permitted.
BatteriesOnly 2S 7.4V Li-Po or 6-cell 7.2V NiMH allowed. No capacity limit. Li-HV must charge to 7.4V max per BRCA rules. Random checks may occur.

Tips for New Racers

  • Start with a 13.5T motor for balanced performance and control.
  • Use the club's control tyres to get the best traction and compliance.
  • Practice smooth throttle and brake control for better lap times.
  • Chat with experienced racers — they’re always happy to help!

On-Track Action

Expect high-flying jumps, quick corners, and tight competition every race night in the buggy class.
